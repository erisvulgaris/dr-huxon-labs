# Visual Identity — Dr. Huxon Labs

## Personality
Dr. Huxon Labs is a premium, plant-based nutrition and supplement brand from India. Its personality is **luxurious, clinical, natural, and modern**. It marries the scientific precision of molecular biochemistry with the organic warmth of plant-based nutrition, conveying clinical authority, uncompromising quality, and high-end wellness.

---

## Typography
We pair two intentional, premium fonts to reinforce the brand's personality without using system defaults:
- **Display Font:** `Fraunces` (a beautiful, expressive serif with high contrast, used for headings to express premium luxury and traditional science).
- **Body & Sans Font:** `Geist Sans` (a highly legible, clean, modern monospace-derived sans-serif, used for UI elements, labels, and paragraph body text).
- **Mono Font:** `Geist Mono` (used for tabular stats, calculator results, and technical metrics to emphasize scientific precision).

## Illustration & Graphics Style
- **Molecular and Hexagonal Textures:** Geometric vector patterns mimicking organic chemistry structures and molecular grids.
- **Glassmorphism Overlay:** Clean card designs using backdrop blurs (`backdrop-filter`) and thin gradient borders that give a floating, high-tech aesthetic.
- **Minimalist Plant Icons:** Thin-line SVG icons representing natural botanicals, FSSAI compliance, lab tests, and clean labels.
- **Isolate Photography:** Gold-rimmed, high-contrast, clinical product isolates placed on ambient radial gradient backdrops.

---

## Animation Language
- **Immersive Spring Physics:** Snappy yet smooth user interface responses for navigation triggers, button click ripples, and card hover scaling.
- **Ambient Aura Drift:** Slow, shifting background radial gradients (20s duration) mimicking the drift of natural light.
- **Animated Number Counter:** Increments numeric stats (protein targets, review scores) using linear-spring interpolation on scroll.
- **Magnetic Interactivity:** Interactive buttons and icons that subtly track the mouse pointer on desktop, combined with micro-particle glowing highlights.

## Inspiration References
1. **Apple Health Web Interface:** For its hyper-clean information card layout, interactive charts, and clinical clarity.
2. **Stripe & Linear:** For their sleek dark modes, micro-animations, glassmorphism containers, and beautiful gradient-border sweeps.
3. **High-end Luxury D2C Supplements (e.g., Ritual, Athletic Greens):** For the warm-ivory base theme, gold typography accents, and strict clinical ingredient transparency layouts.
